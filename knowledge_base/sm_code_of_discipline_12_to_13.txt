PART VI
Code of Discipline

Section 12: Due Process

A pupil / student, who has been accused of any infraction or breach of the Code of Discipline, will be accorded the right to due process before any sanction will be meted.

    For disciplinary offenses, the Head Disciplinarian shall conduct summary proceedings and impose sanctions according to the guidelines set in this handbook.

    For serious disciplinary offenses, the following procedure is observed:
    a. INVESTIGATION. It refers to the act of gathering information relevant to the complaint / case. It also involves the routine questioning of the offending pupil / student and / or witnesses and the searching for physical evidence. The Head Disciplinarian assisted by his / her staff, conducts the investigation.
    b. DETERMINATION OF THE MERIT OF THE CASE. After the investigation, the Head Disciplinarian shall endorse the case to the Vice President, who in turn calls for a meeting with the Student Discipline Committee.
    c. DECISION OF THE STUDENT DISCIPLINE COMMITTEE. The Student Discipline Committee shall deliberate on the complaint / case and recommend a corresponding sanction. The parents / guardian concerned will be informed of any complaint and sanctions that may be enforced on the pupil / student either through a conference or correspondence.

Section 13: Duties of Parents

    Parents are required to attend Parents Orientation/ Quarterly Parent-Teacher Conference and other related activities.

    Parents are to cooperate fully with the teacher if the behavior of the child requires a dialogue.

    Parents/Visitors are not allowed to see their children or any teacher during class hours without permission from the Vice President, and in his/her absence, the Head Disciplinarian.

    Parents are required to wear proper attire as prescribed by the school when entering the campus. Shorts, undershirts, slippers, sleeveless shirts/spaghetti straps are strictly prohibited inside the school campus.
