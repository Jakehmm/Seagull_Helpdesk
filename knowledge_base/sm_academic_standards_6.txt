PART II
Academic Standards

Section 6: Grading System

Grades are representations of the pupil's / student's academic performance in different subject areas.

    Grades are computed at the end of every grading period based on the general guidelines provided for by DepEd with the following levels of assessment:

The assessment shall be done at four levels and shall be weighted as follows: Written Work: 30%; Performance Task: 40% (comprising Product at 15% and Performance-Based Task at 25%); Quarterly Assessment: 30%. 

For online classes, the assessment weight is: Written Work: 25%; Performance Task: 30%; Participation: 15%; Quarterly Assessment: 30%.

    The Kindergarten Department makes use of a Skills Checklist/ Written Assessment to formally document data collected on the pupil's academic skills and developmental traits. The checklist is divided into different domains of development, which are further elaborated into expected skills, attitudes and behaviors. The domains are: Physical, Social and Emotional, Cognitive, Logical and Mathematical and Arts. Each item is assessed and marked using the following rubric:

S - SECURED BEHAVIOR. Behavior is already a strong part of the child's repertoire, displaying it often and in many situations, and becomes the basis for the development of new behaviors.

D - DEVELOPING BEHAVIOR. Behavior is seen regularly or consistently but in its early stages and not yet generalized in all situations.

幼儿评估手册记录幼儿从入园到大班的成长过程，以供家长了解幼儿在园的表现，便于学校做好家园联系工作。本手册的评估方式以五大领域为主，分别是健康、社会、科学、语言、艺术。教师于期中和期末两个阶段有针对性地对幼儿进行五大领域的观察、评估，其中包括笔试及口试，并以优、良、中、差的形式记录幼儿的成长。

    In all written examinations, the score of 60% of the total number of items is equivalent to the passing grade of 75%.

    The passing grade in the report card is 75%

    Academic Honors are categorized as follows:

Academic Honors are: 

For SHS: 95% and above: Great Distinction; 92.5 – 94.999%: High Distinction; 90 – 92.499%: Distinction.

For GS & JHS: 95% and above: With Highest Honors; 92.5 – 94.999%: With High Honors; 90 – 92.499%: With Honors.

优秀生等级
优秀生等级划分如下：
高中部：95分以上为最高荣誉奖；92.5至94.999分为荣誉奖；90至92.499分为优秀奖。
小学/初中部：95分以上为一等奖；92.5至94.999分为二等奖；90至92.499分为三等奖。

    The final grade is obtained using the averaging system.

    The report card (DepEd F-138) is issued to the pupil / student at the end of every grading period or semester.

Kinder

a. The Developmental Checklist is released to the pupils at the end of every semester.

b. The parents/guardians are expected to attend the Parent Teachers Conference (PTC) and receive the Developmental Checklist.

Grade School /High School

a. After the First and Third Grading Periods (GS & JHS); mid-term (SHS), parents / guardians are expected to come to school for the distribution of report cards and the Parent-Teacher Conference (PTC).

b. Parents / guardians must sign and return the proof of receipt to the adviser a day after the issuance of the report card.

c. Tampering with the report card or forging the parent's / guardian's signature is a serious offense that will be dealt with strictly.
d. The Report card for the final grading period at the end of the school year is released only to the pupil/student.
