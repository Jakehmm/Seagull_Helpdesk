PCC School Objectives

The Kindergarten Objectives

PCC Kinder Department aims for the total development of pre-school children into productive, independent and confident learners aware of their cultural heritage. It aims to develop love for learning by providing children with a child-centered environment that promotes exploration and creativity.

The Grade School Objectives

In the light of the school's philosophy of education, vision and mission, the Grade School Department endeavors to help pupils to:

Acquire basic knowledge and develop fundamental skills, values and attitudes in preparation for their secondary education;
Cultivate correct values and put into practice desirable social attitudes;
Communicate effectively and proficiently in oral and written Filipino, English and Chinese;
Develop their critical thinking and creativity skills;
Appreciate their Chinese-Filipino heritage and use it for their total development as individuals;
Imbue the love of country and concern for their fellow countrymen;
Gain awareness of social and environmental concerns in the local, national and global contexts.

The High School Objectives

In the light of the school's philosophy of education, vision and mission, the High School Department endeavors to help students to:

Develop communication skills and critical thinking in preparation for their tertiary education;
Promote their physical and mental health in preparation for coping with real life situations;
Acquire vocational knowledge and skills in preparation for gainful employment and for making intelligent career choice in the future;
Appreciate and cherish their culture, customs and traditions as both Chinese and Filipinos;
Strengthen their heritage as Chinese-Filipinos and deepen their concern and involvement in promoting the growth, development and welfare of the nation;
Develop the ideals of democracy, equality, self-reliance, self-discipline, civic consciousness as well as other values that will promote a healthy, enlightened and morally upright citizenry for national development and progress.

The College Objectives

In the light of the school's philosophy of education, vision and mission, the College Department endeavors to help students to:

Attain excellence in learning through quality program and value integration in every academic discipline;
Equip students with knowledge and skills in modern technology;
Instill quality research projects;
Develop responsible and productive member of the family, school, and society;
Build proactive and innovative leaders;
Provide multi-cultural learning environment through dynamic interaction among members of the academic community;
Promote progressive innovation and change for individual advancement, national development and global competitiveness;
Establish linkages and network among local and international institutions/organizations in the academe and industry;
Adapt dual-learning system through practicum internship and alliance;
Inculcate core values such as diligence, honesty, integrity, respect, courage, compassion and equality.

幼儿园的教育目标：侨中学院幼儿园学龄前孩子的总发展目标是“坚持以孩子为中心”，注重孩子“体、智、德、美、劳”的全面发展，努力培养出“独立自信”、“探索创新”，具有多元文化和中华民族传统特色的新生代。

小学的教育目标：

    学好基础知识，培养基本技能以及正确的价值观，为日后踏入中学铺路；

    培养并应用正确的社会价值观；

    能够使用通顺流利的菲文、英文和华文跟别人沟通；

    培养尖锐以及有创造性的思维能力；

    认同菲律宾华人的优良传统，使得学童得以全面发展；

    灌输爱国主义和民族主义思想；

    关心社会，放眼世界。

中学的教育目标：

    培养交际技能，提供判断能力，为大学教育作准备；

    增强体质，拥有健康的思想，以应付实际的日常生活；

    获取职业知识及技能，以备将来就业及选择事业之需；

    不忘本源，热爱菲中文化与传统；

    巩固菲华民族传统，深切关注并参与促进国家的成长、发展及福利的工作；

    培养民主、平等、自立、自律、自觉及其它正确的观念，成为国家发展及进步正直有为的公民。
