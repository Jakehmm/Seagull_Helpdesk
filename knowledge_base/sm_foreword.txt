Student Handbook
FOREWORD

This handbook contains the different academic policies, which all pupils/students are expected to know and observe. It also contains important guidelines on how the pupils/students should behave on and off-campus. Hence, all pupils/students are expected to read, understand, and abide by the rules and regulations contained in this handbook. Since the school recognizes the indispensable role of the family in nurturing the children, parents/guardians are expected to know and be familiar with the contents of this handbook.
