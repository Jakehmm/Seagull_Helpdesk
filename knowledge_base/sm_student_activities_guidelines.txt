PART V
Student Activities: Programs and Guidelines

Student activities are classified as organizations, clubs, performing groups, and school sports. Participation in student activities is given credit in the deliberation for graduation honors.

Section 1: Organizations

There are two major organizations in PCC. The school paper, Tanglaw for the Manila campus and Scribe/Ang Patnubay for the Caloocan campus, and the Student Council.

Section 2: Clubs

Starting grade four, each PCC student is required to choose from among the list of co-curricular and extra-curricular clubs a club he/she wishes to join for the year. The groups meet regularly during activity class. An activity grade is given once every semester.

Section 3: Performing Groups

Artistically inclined pupils/students are encouraged to participate in the school's performing groups. Performing groups stage performances during school activities. They also represent the school in cultural and arts festivals around the country and abroad from time to time. Performing groups include the Salinlahi Dance Troupe/Footprints, Chinese Dance Troupe, PCC Glee Club, PCC Chinese Orchestra, and the PCC Theater Guild.

Section 4: School Sports

The school offers different competitive sports and athletic activities under the Sports Development Office (SDO) for various interests of the students. The PCC varsity teams are trained by varsity coaches and they participate and represent the school in various off-campus athletic meets, tournaments, and competitions. The following competitive sports are offered: Badminton, Table Tennis, Basketball, Track and Field, Chess, Volleyball, Pep Squad, Wushu, and Swimming.
